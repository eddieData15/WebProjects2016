Duran Duran

Duran Duran are:

Simon LeBon, lead vocal; Roger Taylor, drums and percussion;
Andy Taylor, lead guitar; John Taylor, bass guitar; Nick Rhodes, keyboard.

Discography

  • Duran Duran
    • This self-titled debut album thrust the New Romantic Brit-pop group into the international spotlight.
      Is There Something I Should Know and Girls On Film became the top singles and music videos from this release.
  • Rio
    • This second album from the band firmly established "The Fab Five" as the leading Brit-pop band to emerge in the 80s.
      The title track, along with Hungry Like A Wolf and Save A Prayer, became mainstays on the Billboard charts.
  • Paper Gods
    • This is the band's 14th album which launched in 2015. Notable collaborations include ex-Red Hot Chili Peppers guitarist John Frusciante,
      Nile Rogers, Jonelle Monae and Lindsey Lohan lending guest vocals.Paper Gods preserves the synth-pop sound that has become the group's trademark,
      while bringing divergent music styles that keep Duran Duran's music fresh and relevant among fans of electronic music.
