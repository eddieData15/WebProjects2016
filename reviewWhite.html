VineReview: Cooper's Hawk
Name/Winery Description Price
Cooper's Hawk Type: Unoaked Chardonnay

Vintage: 2015

Color: Pale straw yellow

Pairings: fish, seafood gumbo, pork

Comments: If you're someone who is burned out on "oakey" Chardonnays, this wine is for you. The nose is of citrus and Granny Smith apple with a slightly grassy note. The bright acidity makes this wine suitable for a refreshing summer spritz or a winter sip with salad. 		$17.99
