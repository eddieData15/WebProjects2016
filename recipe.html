Mexican Chorizo Hash

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces raw Mexican chorizo
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup coarsley chopped bottled
    roasted red peppers
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 6-ounce package baby spinach
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 2 1/2 cups refrigerated shredded hash browns
    (such as Simply Potatoes)
  • 4 large eggs (optional)

Directions

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat;
    Add chorizo to the pan; cook 3 minutes until
    browned, stirring to crumble.
  2. Add onion, roasted bell peppers, salt and
    black pepper; cook 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add spinach, stir until spinach wilts; remove
    sausage mixture from pan.
  4. Add oil to pan; swirl to coat.
  5. Add potatoes; cook 8 minutes or until
    bottom is crisp.
  6. Add sausage mixture.
  7. Make 4 egg-size spaces in pan with a spoon.
  8. Crack 1 egg into each space.
  9. Cover and cook 4 minutes or until egg yolks
    are slightly set.

Skip steps 7-9 if excluding eggs.
